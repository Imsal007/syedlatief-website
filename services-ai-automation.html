Frequently Asked

What tools do you use for automation?

We are platform-agnostic but specialize in Zapier, Make (Integromat), and n8n for workflow automation. For AI, we use OpenAI (Custom GPTs), Voiceflow, and Botpress depending on your specific needs and budget.

Do I need to know how to code to manage the systems?

No. We build "no-code" or "low-code" systems specifically so your team can manage them without a developer. We also provide full video documentation and a 1-hour training session upon handover.

How long does a custom build take?

Most standard automations (booking, reminders, CRM sync) take 1–2 weeks. Complex AI chatbots or multi-step portals take 3–4 weeks. We work in agile sprints so you see progress weekly.

Is my business data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, ensure GDPR compliance for all data handling, and never train public AI models on your private business data.
