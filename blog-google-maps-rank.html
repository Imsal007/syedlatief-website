How to Rank #1 on Google Maps in East London (2025 Guide)

Local SEO · East London

Want your business to appear at the top of Google Maps when customers search in East London? You're not alone. Local pack rankings can make or break a small business.

1. Optimise Your Google Business Profile

Your GBP is the single most important factor. Complete EVERY section:

  • Business name (exact real name - no keyword stuffing)
  • Address and postcode (E1, E14, E8, etc.)
  • Phone number and website
  • Hours of operation (including holiday hours)
  • Services and products list
  • Categories (primary + secondary)

2. Get Consistent Local Citations

Your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) must be identical across ALL directories:

  • Yelp
  • Bing Places
  • Thomson Local
  • Yell.com
  • Cylex UK
  • Industry-specific directories

3. Generate Customer Reviews

Reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local pack. Strategy that works:

  • Ask every happy customer (train your staff)
  • Send a follow-up email with direct review link
  • Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
  • Aim for 10+ new reviews per month
🔥 Pro Tip: When responding to reviews, mention local landmarks or keywords. Google reads these!

4. Add Photos Weekly

Businesses with 100+ photos get 5x more engagement. Post:

  • Interior and exterior shots
  • Team photos
  • Product images
  • Customer testimonials (with permission)
  • Behind-the-scenes content

5. Build Local Backlinks

Get other East London websites to link to you:

  • Join local business groups
  • Sponsor community events
  • Get featured in local news
  • Partner with non-competing businesses

6. Optimise Your Website for Local SEO

Create location-specific pages for each area you serve. Include:

  • East London landmarks in content
  • Embedded Google Map
  • Local business schema markup
  • Mobile-friendly design

7. Track and Measure

Use Google Search Console to track:

  • Local keyword rankings
  • Click-through rates
  • Map pack appearances
