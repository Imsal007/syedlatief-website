The Ecommerce Leak

Most online stores lose 70% of potential sales before checkout. Here's why.

🛒

Cart Abandonment

Customers add to cart but leave. Hidden shipping costs, forced account creation, or a confusing checkout process kill conversions.

🐌

Slow Mobile Load

60%+ of traffic is mobile. If your store takes 4+ seconds to load, Google penalizes you and customers bounce to Amazon.

📦

Shipping Nightmares

Manual shipping calculations, wrong zones, and no real-time rates. You either overcharge (lose sales) or undercharge (lose profit).

🔍

Invisible on Google

Product pages lack schema markup, optimized titles, and fast indexing. You rely 100% on paid ads instead of organic search.

Frequently Asked

Shopify or WooCommerce — which is better for me?

Shopify is best for ease of use, hosted security, and rapid scaling (monthly fee applies). WooCommerce is best if you want full ownership, lower monthly costs, and deep WordPress integration. We'll recommend the right platform during your free audit based on your budget and technical comfort.

Do you write product descriptions?

Yes. We offer SEO-optimized product copywriting as an add-on service (£15-£30 per product). If you provide the raw details, we'll craft compelling, search-friendly descriptions that drive sales.

How long does a store build take?

Starter stores launch in 2-3 weeks. Growth stores take 4-5 weeks. Custom enterprise projects take 6-8 weeks depending on complexity and integrations. We provide a clear timeline before starting.

Can you migrate my existing store?

Absolutely. We handle full migrations from platforms like Wix, Squarespace, or Magento to Shopify/WooCommerce. We ensure all products, customers, and order history are transferred securely without downtime.

Do you handle ongoing maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages for updates, security patches, new product uploads, and conversion rate optimization. Check our Web Architecture service page for details.

What payment gateways do you support?

We integrate all major UK gateways: Stripe, PayPal, Klarna, Clearpay, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We ensure PCI compliance and secure checkout flows to maximize customer trust.
