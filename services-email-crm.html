Frequently Asked

Which email platform is best for my business?

For ecommerce (Shopify/WooCommerce), we highly recommend Klaviyo. For B2B, service businesses, and complex automations, ActiveCampaign or HubSpot are best. For simple newsletters on a budget, Mailchimp works well. We'll recommend the right tool during your free audit.

Do you write the email copy?

Yes. Our service includes professional copywriting for all automated flows and newsletter campaigns. We write in your brand voice, optimized for engagement and conversions.

How do you ensure emails don't go to spam?

We handle the technical setup: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records. We also clean your list regularly to remove inactive subscribers and monitor your sender score to ensure high deliverability.

Can you migrate my existing email list?

Absolutely. We handle the full migration process from your current platform (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, etc.) to your new platform, ensuring all tags, segments, and historical data are preserved.

What is the minimum contract length?

There is no minimum. We operate on a rolling monthly basis with 30 days cancellation notice. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not contracts.
