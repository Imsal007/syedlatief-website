Frequently Asked

Is WhatsApp Business API legal and GDPR compliant in the UK?

Yes, when set up correctly. The official Meta API requires explicit user opt-in before you can send marketing messages. We build compliant opt-in flows (via website forms or keyword triggers) and ensure all data is processed according to UK GDPR standards. You also get the green verified tick, building instant trust.

How much does the WhatsApp API cost to run?

Meta charges per "conversation" (a 24-hour window). The first 1,000 service conversations per month are free. After that, it's roughly £0.03-£0.06 per conversation depending on the country. We help you forecast these costs during setup so there are no surprises.

Can the AI handle WhatsApp group chats?

Yes. We can deploy an AI bot into your WhatsApp groups that automatically answers FAQs, removes spam links, welcomes new members, and even polls the group for event RSVPs. It runs 24/7 without you lifting a finger.

What is the difference between a Custom GPT and a standard chatbot?

Standard chatbots use rigid decision trees ("Press 1 for Sales"). Custom GPTs use Large Language Models trained specifically on your business documents. They understand context, handle complex questions naturally, and can be instructed to adopt your exact brand tone.

Do I own the AI models and automations you build?

100%. Once the project is complete and paid for, you own all the code, workflows, API connections, and trained models. We can hand over full admin access, or you can keep us on a lightweight retainer to manage it for you.
