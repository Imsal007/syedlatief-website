Frequently Asked

Why is a full-funnel retainer better than hiring separate freelancers?

Because marketing channels don't work in isolation. Your SEO content should inform your ad copy. Your email data should inform your landing pages. When one team handles everything, the strategy is unified, the data is connected, and you have one person accountable for results.

Do I need all these services to start?

No. We'll audit your business and recommend the right starting point. If you only need SEO and Content to start, we'll begin there. As you grow, we can layer in ads and email marketing seamlessly.

What's the minimum contract length?

We require a 3-month initial commitment to see meaningful results, especially with SEO and content. After that, it's a rolling monthly contract with 30 days cancellation notice.

Who will I be working with?

You'll have a dedicated account manager who coordinates our team of specialists (SEO experts, copywriters, ad buyers, designers). You have one point of contact for everything.

Do you handle the ad spend?

No. You pay Google and Meta directly for your ad spend. Our fee is purely for strategy, management, and optimization. This ensures full transparency over your budget.
