Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026.

Who we are

SAL Digital is operated by Syed Latief, based in London, UK. We provide AI consultancy, digital marketing, and web development services to small businesses across the UK.

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at info@syedlatief.com.

What data we collect

We collect the following information when you interact with our website:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you submit the contact form or book a call via Calendly.
  • Email address when you sign up for the lead generation waitlist.
  • Usage data via any analytics tools we may use in the future.

Why we collect it

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries you send through our contact form.
  • To book and manage discovery calls via Calendly.
  • To send service information you have requested.
  • To improve the website and our services.

How long we keep it

Contact form submissions are kept for 12 months. After that period, we delete or anonymise the data.

Calendly booking data is managed by Calendly per their own privacy policy. We do not store payment data on our servers.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict your data. You can exercise any of these rights by emailing info@syedlatief.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Third party services we use

We use the following third party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Formspree for contact form processing.
  • Calendly for booking management.
  • Cloudflare for hosting and security.
  • Google Fonts for typography.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only for site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies without your explicit consent.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last changed. We recommend checking this page periodically.
