Frequently Asked

Who writes the content?

Our content is written by experienced UK-based writers who understand your industry. We use AI to speed up research and editing, but every piece is reviewed and refined by humans for quality and accuracy.

How long until I see results?

SEO content typically takes 3-6 months to rank. However, you'll see traffic improvements within 60-90 days as Google indexes your new pages. Lead magnets can generate leads immediately.

Do I need to approve content before publishing?

Yes. Every blog post goes through your approval before we publish. We send drafts via Google Docs with comments, and you can request revisions (up to 2 rounds per post included).

What industries do you write for?

We specialize in B2B services, local businesses, professional services (legal, accounting, consulting), trades, healthcare, and e-commerce. If you're in a niche industry, we'll research it thoroughly before writing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We operate on rolling monthly contracts with 30 days cancellation notice. No long-term commitments. We believe in earning your business every month.

Do you handle content publishing?

Yes. We can publish directly to your WordPress, Webflow, or custom website. If you prefer to publish yourself, we'll provide formatted content ready to upload.
