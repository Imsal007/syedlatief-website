Frequently Asked

Do I need to provide the photos and videos?

No. We create custom graphics, source high-quality stock, and write scripts for short-form videos. If you have specific brand assets or photos you want us to use, we'll integrate them seamlessly.

Which platforms should I be on?

It depends on your audience. B2B and professional services thrive on LinkedIn. Visual brands (salons, food, retail) do best on Instagram and TikTok. We'll audit your current presence and recommend the best 1-3 platforms.

How long until I see results?

Social media is a long-term play. You'll see improved consistency and engagement within the first 30 days. Meaningful follower growth and inbound leads typically compound over 3-6 months of consistent strategy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We don't believe in locking you into 12-month contracts. We operate on a rolling monthly basis with a simple 30-day cancellation notice.

Do you run paid ads?

Organic social media and paid ads are separate services. If you want to boost your best-performing organic posts with paid ads, check out our Paid Advertising service page.
