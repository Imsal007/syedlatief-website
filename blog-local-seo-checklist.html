Local SEO Checklist: 10 Steps to Dominate Your Area in 2025

SEO Strategy

Stop guessing. Use this checklist to systematically improve your local SEO. Tick off each step as you go.

✅ Step 1: Claim & Verify Google Business Profile

If you haven't done this, stop everything. Go to business.google.com and claim your profile. Verification takes 3-5 days by postcard.

✅ Step 2: Complete Every GBP Section

Fill in: business hours, services, products, description, attributes (e.g., "women-owned", "free wifi").

✅ Step 3: Add 10+ Photos

Include: exterior, interior, team, products, team at work. Add 2-3 new photos weekly.

✅ Step 4: Get 5+ Reviews

Ask happy customers for reviews. Send a direct link. Respond to every review within 24 hours.

✅ Step 5: Fix NAP Consistency

Your Name, Address, Phone number must be IDENTICAL across: website, Google, Yelp, Facebook, directories.

✅ Step 6: Build Local Citations

Get listed on: Yelp, Bing Places, Thomson Local, Yell.com, Cylex UK, FreeIndex, Scoot.

✅ Step 7: Create Location Pages

If you serve multiple areas (e.g., E1, E14, E8), create a dedicated page for each.

✅ Step 8: Add Local Schema Markup

LocalBusiness schema helps Google understand your location. Add it to your website header.

✅ Step 9: Build Local Backlinks

Get links from: local news sites, chamber of commerce, business directories, community pages.

✅ Step 10: Track Your Rankings

Use Google Search Console to monitor "near me" keywords. Check your Map Pack position monthly.
