Frequently Asked

Do I pay you the ad spend or Google directly?

You pay Google/Meta directly with your own credit card. We never touch your ad budget. Our fee is purely for management. This means full transparency — you see exactly where every penny goes.

How much should I spend on ads per month?

For local businesses, we recommend starting with £500-£1,500/month ad spend. For national campaigns or e-commerce, £3,000-£10,000/month. We'll advise on the right budget based on your goals and market.

How quickly will I see results?

Google Search Ads can generate leads within 48 hours of launch. Meta Ads typically take 7-14 days to optimize. We focus on quick wins first, then scale what works over 30-90 days.

Do you lock me into long contracts?

No. We operate on rolling monthly contracts with 30 days cancellation notice. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not contracts.

What industries do you work with?

We specialize in local services (trades, salons, clinics), professional services (legal, accounting), e-commerce, and B2B. If you're in a regulated industry, we have experience with compliance requirements.

Will I own the ad accounts?

Yes, 100%. All ad accounts are created in your business name. You have full access and ownership. If we part ways, you keep everything — campaigns, data, and history.
