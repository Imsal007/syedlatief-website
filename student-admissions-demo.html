Automated Workflow: Home Students (Funded Study)

For UK residents applying for government-funded courses, Advanced Learner Loans, or university degrees.

1. Instant Eligibility Check

Student enters postcode and course interest. System instantly checks if they qualify for full funding, partial loans, or bursaries.

📄

2. Automated Document Chasing

System requests GCSE/A-Level transcripts and ID. If a document is missing 3 days before the deadline, it automatically texts the student and their parent/guardian.

💷

3. Student Finance Integration

Auto-generates the correct SFE/SLC application forms pre-filled with student data. Sends step-by-step video guides on how to link their account.

4. Offer & Enrollment Automation

When the university sends an offer, the portal instantly notifies the student, auto-accepts conditional offers, and triggers the enrollment checklist.

Automated Workflow: International Students

For overseas students requiring CAS letters, Tier 4/Student Visas, and international fee processing.

🛂

1. Visa & English Requirement Check

Student selects their home country. System instantly shows exact visa requirements, English language scores needed (IELTS/PTE), and financial maintenance thresholds.

🔒

2. Secure Passport & Bank Statement Upload

GDPR-compliant encrypted portal for sensitive documents. Auto-translates foreign transcripts and verifies bank statement dates for visa compliance.

📜

3. CAS Letter Generation Trigger

Once fees are paid and documents verified, the system automatically alerts the university to issue the CAS letter and emails it to the student with visa application links.

️

4. Pre-Arrival & Airport Pickup

Automated "Welcome to the UK" sequence: sends accommodation links, airport pickup booking forms, and orientation schedules 2 weeks before arrival.
