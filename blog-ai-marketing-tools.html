AI Marketing for Small Businesses: 5 Free Tools That Actually Work

AI Marketing

You don't need a technical background or a big budget to use AI in your marketing. These 5 free tools can save you 10+ hours per week.

🤖 1. ChatGPT (Free)

Write emails, social captions, blog outlines, and ad copy. Just tell it what you need. Example: "Write a Facebook post for my East London coffee shop announcing a new latte flavour."

📧 2. Mailchimp's AI Assistant (Free tier)

Generates email subject lines and content based on your brand voice. Saves hours on newsletter creation.

📸 3. Canva AI (Free)

Generate social media graphics, remove backgrounds, and write caption ideas. One-click resizing for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn.

🎙️ 4. Otter.ai (Free)

Record and transcribe meetings, customer calls, or brainstorming sessions. Get summaries and action items automatically.

📊 5. Google Analytics 4 (Free) + AI Insights

GA4 automatically surfaces insights like "Your mobile traffic increased 40% from East London last week." No data analysis needed.

How to Start Today

Step 1: Pick ONE tool from this list.
Step 2: Spend 30 minutes learning it.
Step 3: Use it for one task this week.
Step 4: Add another tool next week.

AI won't replace you, but a business owner using AI will outpace one who doesn't.
