Frequently Asked

How long does a website build take?

Most projects launch in 3-4 weeks. This includes design approval, development, content, testing, and launch. Rush builds (2 weeks) are available for an additional fee.

Do you write the content or do I?

You provide the raw content (about you, services, etc.) and we optimize it for SEO and conversions. If you need full copywriting, we offer that as an add-on service (£400-800).

What platform do you build on?

We're platform-agnostic but typically use WordPress, Webflow, or custom HTML/CSS depending on your needs. We choose the best tool for your specific goals and budget.

Will my website rank on Google?

We build SEO foundations (speed, mobile, schema, meta tags) but ranking requires ongoing effort. Our Growth Partnership includes monthly SEO optimization to climb rankings over time.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes. We build on platforms with easy editors (WordPress, Webflow) and provide a 1-hour training session. Most clients update text and images themselves without touching code.

What if I don't like the design?

We include 2 rounds of revisions in the Foundation package. We also show you wireframes and mockups before building, so there are no surprises. 100% of our clients approve the final design.
