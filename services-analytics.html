Frequently Asked

Do I need to give you access to my website?

Yes, we need admin or editor access to your website (WordPress, Shopify, etc.) to install the GTM container code. For GTM and GA4 itself, you grant us "Editor" access via your Google account. We never ask for your personal login passwords.

What is the difference between GA4 and Universal Analytics?

Universal Analytics (UA) was retired by Google in July 2023. GA4 is the new standard. It tracks events instead of sessions, uses AI to fill data gaps, and integrates much better with Google Ads. If you're still using UA, you are flying blind.

Will this fix my low conversion rate?

Analytics doesn't fix conversions directly, but it tells you *exactly* where users are dropping off. Once we identify the broken steps in your funnel (e.g., mobile checkout failing), we can recommend specific fixes to improve your conversion rate.

Do you offer monthly reporting?

The setup is a one-time fee. However, if you want us to monitor your data, provide monthly insights, and suggest optimizations, we offer a lightweight "Data Watch" retainer for £150/month. Most clients just use the dashboard we build.

Is this GDPR compliant for UK businesses?

Yes. We implement GA4 Consent Mode v2, which respects user cookie choices while still allowing Google to model conversion data. We also ensure IP anonymization is active and data retention settings are configured correctly.
